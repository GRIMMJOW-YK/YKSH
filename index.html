INTERSTELLAR: A Cosmic Journey

About the Film

"Interstellar" is a science fiction film where a team of astronauts travels through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet for humanity, as Earth is facing environmental collapse.

The story focuses on ex-NASA pilot Joseph Cooper, who leaves his family to embark on this perilous journey, driven by the hope of saving the human race.

In a dystopian future, Earth is ravaged by blight and dust storms, making it nearly uninhabitable. Joseph Cooper, a former NASA pilot and engineer, now a farmer, discovers a NASA base hidden near his home. He is recruited to pilot the spacecraft Endurance through a newly discovered wormhole, hoping to find a suitable planet for humanity's survival.

The mission involves traversing the wormhole, encountering extreme time dilation near a black hole, and facing difficult decisions that test the limits of human endurance and love.

Sachein: A Timeless Classic

"Sachein" is a Tamil romantic comedy film starring Vijay and Genelia, directed by John Mahendran. The movie tells the story of Sachein, a college student who falls for Shalini, a practical-minded girl who initially feigns indifference.

A key plot point involves Sachein trying to win her over, with a 30-day challenge. The film also features Bipasha Basu, Vadivelu, and others in supporting roles.

"Sachein" was re-released in theaters in April 2025 to celebrate its 20th anniversary, allowing a new generation to experience this beloved classic.
